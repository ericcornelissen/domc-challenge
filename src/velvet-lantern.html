DOM Clobbering Challenge #3

Your goal: Clobber $form.domc

Lastly, you use DOM Clobbering to add and override properties of <form> nodes, which is the goal of this challenge. Like document, <form> nodes can only be clobbered by certain elements using the name attribute. On top of that the element that are "owned" by the form (usually children). The element must be one of <button>, <fieldset>, <img>, <input>, <object>, <output>, <select>, or <textarea>.
