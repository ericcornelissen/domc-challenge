DOM Clobbering Challenge #4

Your goal: Clobber $form.domc, again.

In the previous challenge you clobbered a <form> by inserting an element inside it. In this challenge you can insert an element after the <form>. Recall that to clobber a <form> the clobbering element needs to be owned by it.

If you need some help, look at the attributes list for <input> elements.
